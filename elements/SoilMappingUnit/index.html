    Class: SoilMappingUnit

    A Soil Mapping Unit (SMU) represents a distinct area with relatively homogeneous

    soil characteristics. Each SMU may contain multiple soil types with their

    proportional shares.

    URI: hwsd2:SoilMappingUnit
